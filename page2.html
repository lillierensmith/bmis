Homemade Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Your favorite sauce

Instructions:

  1. Make a mound with the flour, create a well in the center, and add eggs, salt, and oil.
  2. Gradually mix the flour into the eggs until a dough forms.
  3. Knead for 10 minutes, then let it rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Roll out and cut into desired shapes.
  5. Cook in boiling salted water for 2-4 minutes.
  6. Toss in the sauce of your choice.

Olive Oil Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • Zest of 1 lemon

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk olive oil and sugar until smooth. Add eggs one at a time.
  3. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt in another bowl.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients and milk to the wet mixture. Stir in lemon zest.
  5. Line bottom of your cake pan with parchment paper and grease the sides with butter or oil
  6. Pour into the pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  7. Garnish with powdered sugar and serve warm.
